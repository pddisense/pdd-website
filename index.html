Connecting volunteers with researchers.

Private Data Donor is a research project whose goal is to collect web search queries,
in order to identify outbreaks of infectious diseases much earlier than ever before.

Web searches as a tool to advance research.

Every second, about 63,000 search queries are handled by Google worldwide. This data is a unique opportunity for researchers to detect and monitor the spread of infectious diseases, using early-warning signals.

Privacy-by-design.

Only aggregated data is collected, before being further encrypted, meaning that search queries cannot be linked back to individuals. Moreover, users are in full control of their data and can optionally review and modify what is collected before it is sent to our servers.
