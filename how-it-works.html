--- layout: base title: How it works ---
 

You install the Chrome browser extension.

 

You join collection campaigns you are interested in contributing to.

 

Once a day, your browser automatically sends to our server the number of times that you looked for a list of particular queries, related to health monitoring. For example, we will be interested in monitoring the "flu" query, but not that much in the "royal wedding" query.

Your data is aggregated and encrypted in such a way that we can only learn the total number of times each keyword was searched every day, but we cannot link data back to individuals.

 

We use the collected data to predict and monitor the spread of infectious diseases.

 

The browser extension lets you remain in control of your privacy. We do not monitor your search queries if you are in the "Incognito mode". You can also at any time delete some of your browsing history.
